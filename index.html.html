THE MAHINDRA THAR IS DESIGNED BY Mahindra Thar Ramkripa Ananthan The Mahindra Thar is a compact, four-wheel drive, off-road SUV manufactured by Indian automaker Mahindra and Mahindra Ltd. The Thar is designed to be a rugged and capable vehicle that can handle any terrain with ease. It is available with a choice of petrol and diesel engines and comes in both 2-wheel-drive and 4-wheel-drive configurations.Named after the Thar Desert, the Mahindra Thar was first introduced in 2010 as a modernized version of the Mahindra Legend, which was based on the Mahindra MM540, a vehicle that was in production in India since the 1980s.
